About Insignia

GA simple jQuery plug-in that lets high level computer users keep track of their learning on their own dashboard or blog so that they are motivated to keep learning even if they turned off their email notifications for each school l.

STEP 1

<!--latest jQuery-->
<script src="js/jquery-2.1.1.min.js"> </script>
<!-javascript-->
<script src="dist/js/jquery.insignia.js"></script>
<!--insignia css-->
< link href="dist/css/insignia.css" rel="stylesheet">

STEP 2. HTML MARKUP

Replace "user_name" with your name!
<div class="insignia" data-treehouse="user_name" data-codeschool="user_name"></div>
